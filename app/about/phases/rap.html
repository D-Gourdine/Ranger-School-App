RAP Week

RAP (Ranger Assessment Phase) Week is the first week of Training, which consists of 5 different events:

  1. Ranger Tactical Tasks (RTTs):
    1. Maintain an M249 Machine Gun
    2. Clear, Load, Reduce Stoppage, Unload, and Clear an M249 Machine Gun
    3. Maintain an M240B Machine Gun
    4. Clear, Load, Reduce Stoppage, Unload, and Clear an M249 Machine Gun
    5. Employ/Recover an M18A1 Claymore Mine
    6. Communicate by Tactical Radio (ASIP)
    7. Communicate by MBITR
  2. Ranger Physical Fitness Test (RPFT)
    1. Min. 49 Push-Ups in 2 minutes
    2. Min. 59 Sit-Ups in 2 minutes
    3. Max. Time of 40 minutes on 5 mile run
    4. Min. 6 Chin-Ups (palms facing inward)
  3. Land Navigation
  4. 12-Mile Foot March (35-lbs dry)
  5. Combat Water Survival Assessment
    1. 40' Log Walk Rope Drop
    2. 75' Suspension Traverse
    3. Equipment Removal / 15m Swim
