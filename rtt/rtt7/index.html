RTT 7: Communicate by MBITR

Tasks:

  1. Place MBITR into operation.
  2. Load a frequency and properly enter the net.

Test Conditions:

    Given an MBITR (AN/PRC-148), antenna, hand mic, and battery.

Test Standards:

    Properly assemble the MBITR and place into operation, correctly load frequency, and settings and enter the net within 4 minutes.
